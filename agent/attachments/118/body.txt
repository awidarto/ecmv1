SOA in the API World - Facades, Transactions, Stateless Services . . .

Thursday, May 2, 11:00am PT / 2:00pm ET

In a recent webcast, we established the imperative for APIs to evolve
a SOA that's just not flexible enough to meet the requirements of today's
projects. So what does that entail? You'll need to design a facade for your
services, ensure services are stateless, handle complex, stateful
transactions, and more.

Join Brian Pagano and Greg Brail for a deep dive into some top
considerations for evolving your SOA to expose functionality to meet the
demands of business in the growing app economy.
